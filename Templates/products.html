Psyllium Husk

"Pure fiber for a smoother digestion and better health."

Description:

Psyllium Husk is the fibrous outer layer of the Plantago ovata seeds, known for its incredible health benefits. This natural product is packed with soluble fiber that supports digestion, promotes heart health, helps with weight management, and aids in regulating blood sugar levels. Psyllium Husk’s ability to form a gel-like consistency when mixed with water makes it an ideal supplement for improving bowel movements and ensuring smooth digestive processes.

Key Benefits:

  • Supports Digestive Health: Helps regulate bowel movements and eases constipation.
  • Promotes Heart Health: Contributes to cholesterol management and overall cardiovascular health.
  • Aids Weight Management: Its high fiber content promotes a feeling of fullness, reducing unnecessary cravings.
  • Blood Sugar Regulation: Assists in balancing blood sugar levels, making it a valuable supplement for diabetic individuals.

How to Use:

  • Mix with Water: Stir a tablespoon of psyllium husk into a glass of water. Let it swell for a few minutes before drinking.
  • Smoothies: Add to your smoothies for an extra fiber boost.
  • Baking: A key ingredient in gluten-free baking to add fiber and improve texture.

Important Tips:

  • Always consume with plenty of water to avoid digestive issues and ensure the husk swells properly.
  • Start with a small amount to assess your body’s reaction, then gradually increase intake.

Sowing and Harvesting Info:

  • Sowing Time: August to September.
  • Harvesting Time: February to April.

Market Availability:

Fresh Husk Available: March to May.

Nutritional Information (per serving, approx. 1 tbsp):

  • Calories: 20 kcal
  • Fiber: 5g
  • Carbs: 5g (Mostly fiber, no net carbs)

Psyllium Husk Powder

"Fine powder, mighty fiber - a simple way to better health."

Description:

Psyllium Husk Powder is a finely ground, nutrient-rich powder derived from the husks of Plantago ovata seeds. Known for its exceptional concentration of soluble fiber, this powder offers an effortless way to boost digestive health, manage weight, support heart function, and regulate blood sugar levels. When mixed with water, it forms a smooth gel that gently cleanses the digestive tract and promotes satiety, making it a versatile and effective supplement for daily health maintenance.

Key Benefits:

  • Promotes Digestive Wellness:Softens stool and supports regular bowel movements.
  • Supports Heart Health:Assists in lowering cholesterol levels naturally.
  • Aids Weight Management:Creates a feeling of fullness, helping to reduce appetite and control portions.
  • Helps Balance Blood Sugar: Slows digestion and stabilizes glucose absorption after meals.

How to Use:

  • Mix into Water or Juices:Stir a teaspoon of psyllium husk powder into a glass of water or your favorite juice, and drink immediately.
  • Boost Smoothies:Add a small amount to smoothies for a fiber-rich upgrade.
  • Baking:Ideal for gluten-free recipes — improves texture and fiber content in bread, muffins, and pancakes.

Important Tips:

  • Always consume with plenty of water to avoid digestive issues and ensure the husk swells properly.
  • Start with a small amount to assess your body’s reaction, then gradually increase intake.

Sowing and Harvesting Info:

  • Sowing Time: August to September.
  • Harvesting Time: February to April.

Market Availability:

Fresh Powder Available:March to May.

Nutritional Information (per serving, approx. 1 tbsp):

  • Calories: 15 kcal
